g1

g1 shows each individuals progress between the two treatments in a course, split up by groups based on grades received from an end of year test. We see that A grade students show the most increase in ability from the treatment and C grade students show the opposite. Theyre interesting groups to compare because their scores before the treatment were quite similar (Pre), however after the treatment (Post) they deviate. This suggests that the same treatment can have different effects based on the grade an individual gets (or is set to get) and that there is something else going on.

Small decreasing trend between change in confidence and change in ability. Negative numbers for both change in confidence and ability indicate loss post treatment and vice versa. A decreasing trend suggests that as ability is lost, confidence grows hinting as a possible observation of the Duning-Kruger effect. Need further statistical analyses to untangle this further.
